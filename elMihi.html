Stoned Ape Crew 11/17/2022

by elMihi

Stoned Ape Crew is a project with a 4200 supply that came out in late November 21'
and had a mint price of 0.69 SOL (SOL was 200$).

What Is Stoned Ape Crew
Stoned Ape Crew is a utility/community based project.
The main driver behind the project is the OG community.

Utility
This is one of the bigger OG projects (1 year anniversary is in 2 weeks) so a lot has happened since they first minted, I'll try to keep it as short as possible.
The native token is called $PUFF (0.01$) and can be earned via staking.
Staking is a bit all over the place in regards to emissions and halvings so I'll give you the vanilla numbers.
Chimpions: earn 10.2 $PUFF daily. Awakened: 20.4 $PUFF. Shockwaved: 40.8 $PUFF.
These are the initial numbers, they do NOT include 2 halvings of 32% and 42%.
There were 2 types of "upgrades": Retreats (V1) and the Awakening (V2).
Retreats (V1) - use $PUFF to get a new role and earn more via staking, depending on the amount of $PUFF
you burned you hade a higher or lower chance of getting the role (1420 $PUFF was the 100% limit)
Awakening (V2) - same concept use $PUFF to unlock 2 versions, an animated & a static one tied to the same NFT.
ALL BLUE - was the projects play on being and incubator and a launchpad but it failed and the team has discontinued the utility.
The main area the team is looking to expand now is the payments sector with their PayLeaf.
PayLeaf enables customers to pay with their mobile phone at any retail location that accepts PayLeaf in a peer2peer way.

Overview
Honestly there is so much more to talk about like Nuked apes, Magic J's, CBD's, etc... but its hard to fill 1 year of events in a short text.
I want to add just one more thing, SAC was considered one of the best bluechips back in Q1 of 22 but the main "downfall" was the token.
The ATH in price was 0.8$ and while the project kept coming out with new utilities and features the token inevitably saw a big decline and is now worth 0.01$.
Just goes to show that the token price of a project can make or break a project, sadly due to whale games and p&d it usually turns out bad.
The team has never stopped building and adding value but the huge drop in the token price was just too much.

Listed - 80
Staked - 90%+
Current price - 32 SOL



Why are NFT floors "pumping" right now ? 11/15/2022

by elMihi

And no the answer is not the bull run is back
We went over the concept behind the $ value of NFTs yesterday, which is the main reason behind this "pump" (re-calibration) in price.
SOL/USDC has dumped 70% in the past week so the underlining $ value of NFTs is down bad, the current "pump" is only trying to re-establish old $ values.
In simpler terms more SOL is required to reach old $ value floors.

Examples
y00ts = pervious floor 70 SOL (sol 30$) - new floor 101 SOL (sol 14$) = net difference negative 700$
ABC = pervious floor 22 SOL (sol 30$) - new floor 29 SOL (sol 14$) = net difference negative 250$
OK bears = pervious floor 35 SOL (sol 30$) - new floor 46 SOL (sol 14$) = net difference negative 400$

Even though it looks like all fp's are pumping and new highs/profits are being made in reality we are still down bad in $ terms.

by elMihi

Meta Tattoo Club is a project with a 8888 supply that came out in late October and had a mint price of 2 SOL
(SOL was 28$).

What Is Meta Tattoo Club
Meta Tattoo Club is an IRL based project.
The main driver behind the project is the IRL utility.

Utility
The native token is called $INK (no LP atm) and can be earned by staking.
Staking has two forms: Shop staking and Drink the Ink.
Shop staking is a normal version of vanilla staking while Drink the Ink is a gamefied version where you can lvl up your MTC and earn more $INK.
$INK can/will be used in a couple of ways like:
- IRL tattoos - use $INK to pay for IRL tattoos
- Virtual tattoos - use $INK to get your avatar tattooed, first tattoo shop in the Sandbox
- Ink vial - use $INK to purchase an Ink Vial, lvl up your Ink Vial to lvl 5 in order to upgrade your NFT and earn more $INK daily while staking.
- Merch and giveaways

Overview
One of the team members & globally recognized tattoo artist Andreas Ishak is the owner of Saints Touch tattoo studio in Gothenburg, Sweden.
Every month one lucky holder will be getting flown out to Sweden & will receive an 8hr tattoo session, airplane flights, hotel accommodation, spending cash & will be able to bring their +1.
The team started construction of their second shop in Canggu, Bali and is looking to expand into California and Western Canada.
